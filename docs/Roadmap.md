# Roadmap

The following features are planned for upcoming releases:

- **Many more templates for actions, reactions, free actions, and passive actions**
- **Generation of Complex Hazards with routines**
- **Support for vehicle generation**
- **Improved GUI settings with configurable folder locations** _(Foundry VTT)_
- **Integration with third-party modules such as PF2e Subsystems and Stochastic, Fantastic!** _(Foundry VTT)_
